Ngorongoro Conservation Area

Northern Tanzania

Coordinates: 3.1618°S, 35.5570°E

Size: 8,292 km² (3,202 sq mi)

Crater Diameter: 20 km (12 miles)

Famous for: The world's largest inactive volcanic caldera, home to over 25,000 large animals including lions, elephants, rhinos, leopards, and buffalo.

UNESCO World Heritage Site since 1979

Elevation: 1,800m (5,900ft) at crater rim

Experience Africa's "Garden of Eden" - a natural amphitheater teeming with wildlife!
